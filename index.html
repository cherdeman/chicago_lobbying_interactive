Mapping Lobbyist Influence on Chicago City Council

Use the map and tree diagram below to explore the interactions between the clients, i.e. companies and organizations that hire lobbyists, the lobbyists themselves, and Chicago's 50 Aldermen. The map shows Chicago's 50 wards, colored by the amount donated to the alderman by lobbyists in 2018. Click on the interactive tree to explore the links between aldermen, lobbyists, and clients.
